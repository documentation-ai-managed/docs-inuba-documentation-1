## Get Started in Minutes

Set up your Inuba Documentation account and launch your first space quickly. Follow these steps to register, create a space, and add your initial document. You complete the process in under five minutes.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a GitHub account for seamless integration with repositories later.
</Callout>

## Sign Up for an Account

Choose your preferred method to create an account.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    Visit `https://app.inuba.com/signup` and enter your email address, password, and display name. Verify your email to activate the account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">
    Click the GitHub button on the signup page. Authorize Inuba to access your public repositories for future docs syncing.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="google">
    Select the Google option and sign in with your Google account. No additional verification required.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete the Quickstart Workflow

Follow these sequential steps to build your first documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**. Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and select a starter template such as "API Reference" or "Product Guide". Click **Create**.

    <Image
      src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1460925895917-afdab827c52f?w=800&fit=crop"
      alt="Creating a new documentation space in Inuba"
      width="800"
      height="450"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Space Settings" icon="settings">
    In your new space, go to **Settings > General**. Set the space slug to `my-project-docs` and brand color to `#3B82F6`. Save changes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Document" icon="file-text">
    Navigate to **Documents > New Document**. Name it `quickstart.mdx` and paste the following MDX content.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX">
      ```mdx
      ---
      title: Welcome
      description: Your first document.
      ---

      ## Hello World

      This is your Inuba documentation space.
      ```
    </CodeGroup>

    Preview and publish the document.
  </Step>
</Steps>
